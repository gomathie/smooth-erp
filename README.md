# SmoothPOS: Advanced Point of Sale and Inventory Management System

SmoothPOS is a modern web-based Point of Sale (POS) and inventory management system developed using PHP and MySQL. Designed as a practical business solution and learning project, it provides a comprehensive platform for managing products, sales transactions, customers, and inventory operations through an intuitive interface.

The system enables businesses to efficiently process sales, manage stock levels, and generate detailed reports for better decision-making. It maintains product information, categories, customer records, and transaction history, ensuring accurate tracking of day-to-day business activities.

SmoothPOS features a role-based access system with separate panels for administrators and staff members. Administrators have full control over system operations, including inventory management, user administration, sales monitoring, and reporting. Staff members are provided with limited access, allowing them to manage customer information, create sales transactions, and view relevant records based on their assigned permissions.

Key features of SmoothPOS include inventory management, customer management, sales tracking, product categorization, transaction processing, and comprehensive sales reporting. The application is designed to reflect real-world retail operations, making it suitable for small and medium-sized businesses as well as academic and personal development projects.

With its user-friendly design and practical functionality, SmoothPOS offers an efficient solution for streamlining sales operations while maintaining accurate inventory and customer records.
